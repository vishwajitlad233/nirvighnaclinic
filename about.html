About Dr. Prasad Limkar

BHMS | CCMP | PGDEMS — Family Physician & Child Care

Dr. Prasad Limkar

BHMS, CCMP, PGDEMS | Family Physician & Child Care

BHMS CCMP PGDEMS

Dr. Prasad Limkar is a highly respected family physician based in Savedi, Ahmednagar (Ahilyanagar). With dedicated service to the community, he has earned the trust and affection of families in the region.

After completing his Bachelor of Homeopathic Medicine and Surgery (BHMS), Dr. Limkar successfully completed his CCMP and pursued a Post Graduate Diploma in Emergency Medical Services (PGDEMS) to broaden his expertise. This unique combination allows him to offer both gentle, holistic healing and confident emergency care.

His approach to medicine is rooted in empathy — he believes in listening to each patient, understanding their lifestyle, and creating personalized treatment plans that work. Whether it's a child's first vaccination or managing a senior citizen's chronic condition, Dr. Limkar treats every patient like family.
