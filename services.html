Comprehensive Healthcare for Your Family

From preventive care to specialized treatments, we offer a wide range of medical services under one roof.

Family Medicine

Comprehensive primary healthcare services for patients of all ages. We provide routine health check-ups, diagnosis and treatment of common illnesses, preventive care, and health counseling for the entire family.

  • ✓ General health check-ups
  • ✓ Fever, cold, infections treatment
  • ✓ Preventive health screening
  • ✓ Elder care consultations

Child Care & Pediatric Services

Dedicated healthcare for infants, children, and adolescents. We ensure your child's healthy growth and development with regular monitoring and age-appropriate care.

  • ✓ Vaccination & immunization
  • ✓ Growth & development monitoring
  • ✓ Nutrition & diet guidance
  • ✓ Common childhood illnesses

Health Check-up Camps

We regularly organize free and affordable health camps in the community for early detection of diseases and spreading health awareness among local families.

  • ✓ Blood sugar & BP screening
  • ✓ Eye & dental check-ups
  • ✓ Women's health camps
  • ✓ Senior citizen health drives

Chronic Disease Management

Specialized long-term management of chronic conditions with regular monitoring, medication adjustment, lifestyle guidance, and patient education.

  • ✓ Diabetes management
  • ✓ Blood Pressure control
  • ✓ Thyroid treatment
  • ✓ Lifestyle & diet planning

Minor Procedures

Safe, professional minor surgical procedures performed at the clinic with proper sterilization and care. No need to visit a hospital for small procedures.

  • ✓ Wound cleaning & stitching
  • ✓ Abscess drainage
  • ✓ Ear wax removal
  • ✓ Nail procedures
