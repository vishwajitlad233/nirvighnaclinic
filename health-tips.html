Stay Healthy, Stay Informed

Practical health advice from Dr. Prasad Limkar to keep your family healthy

Child Health

Importance of Timely Vaccination for Children

Vaccinations are one of the most effective ways to protect your child from serious diseases like polio, measles, hepatitis, and more. Every child should follow the vaccination schedule recommended by their doctor.

💡 Tip: Keep a vaccination card and set reminders for the next dose.

Diabetes

Managing Diabetes with Diet & Lifestyle

A balanced diet is key to controlling blood sugar levels. Include whole grains (jowar, bajra, nachni), green vegetables, dal, and fresh fruits in your daily meals. Avoid refined sugar, maida, and processed foods.

💡 Tip: Walk for 30 minutes daily and check your blood sugar regularly.

Seasonal Health

Preventing Monsoon Illnesses

During monsoon season, waterborne diseases like dengue, malaria, typhoid, and gastroenteritis increase. Drink boiled or filtered water, avoid street food, keep surroundings clean, and use mosquito repellents.

💡 Tip: If fever persists for more than 2 days, consult your doctor immediately.

Nutrition

Healthy Eating Habits for Children

Good nutrition in childhood builds the foundation for a healthy life. Include milk, eggs, fruits, green vegetables, and nuts in your child's diet. Limit packaged snacks, chocolates, and cold drinks.

💡 Tip: Make mealtime fun and involve children in food preparation.

Blood Pressure

Understanding & Controlling High Blood Pressure

High blood pressure (hypertension) is a silent killer that increases risk of heart attack and stroke. Regular monitoring, reduced salt intake, regular exercise, and stress management are key to controlling it.

💡 Tip: Check your blood pressure at least once a month after age 30.
